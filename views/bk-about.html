About

Blue Kitchen, Inc. is a non-profit founded in our own blue kitchen in the Spring of 2024. We decided on this name because this is generally where we gather to connect and share nourishment for body and mind.

The ideas behind Blue Kitchen were spawned during the 2020 COVID-19 pandemic with its isolating disruption to our lives. During this time, we were presented with a unique opportunity to our break routines, reflect on and reevaluate how and where we were spending our time and realign ourselves with our goals. It also brought a staunch awareness (a.k.a. a reality check) of how quickly life can change and how dependent we are upon each other and society.

During this time of isolation and uncertainty, we learned to be unapologetically creative and resourceful and in this, we learned how to convert abiguity and uncertainty into meaningfulness and personal growth.

Blue Kitchen, Inc. became an umbrella for all things we learned, hope to learn and wish to share. Welcome to the Blue Kitchen!
